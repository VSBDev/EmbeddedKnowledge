# Recover a half-life from two observations

:::{check}
:id: check-transfer-half-life

In an illustrative teaching model, a drug's plasma concentration follows $A(t)=A_0(1/2)^{t/h}$, where $h>0$ is its half-life. After 14 hours, the ratio $A/A_0$ is 0.20. Estimate $h$.

Show the positive ratio, logarithmic step, sign check, and reverse substitution before continuing.
:::

Record a complete attempt before opening the next scene, which begins with worked feedback. The task estimates a parameter inside the stated illustrative model; it does not represent a specific drug or patient and does not establish that any measured system follows exact exponential decay.
