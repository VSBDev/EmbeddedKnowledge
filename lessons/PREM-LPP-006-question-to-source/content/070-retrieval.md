## Feedback after your attempt

The missing steps are **answer product** and **concept blocks**. A complete reconstruction asks:

1. What do I need to know, and what will I do with the answer?
2. What answer product is needed now?
3. Which source type or discovery environment performs that function?
4. Which distinct concepts determine relevance?
5. What did the translation and small sample show, and which one block should change?

The ranking explanation should distinguish retrieval relevance from appraisal. If you named only keywords, return to the source-purpose scene. If you named a source but could not justify its function, rewrite the three source-choice sentences.
