# Choose the relation from the labels

## 1. Scale a shape

A rectangle's linear dimensions are multiplied by 1.5. What happens to its area?

**Feedback:** Area is multiplied by $1.5^2=2.25$.

## 2. Complete a right triangle

The hypotenuse is 10 and one leg is 6. Find the other leg.

**Feedback:** $\sqrt{10^2-6^2}=8$. The leg is below the hypotenuse.

## 3. Convert angle units

Convert $135^\circ$ to radians.

**Feedback:** $135(\pi/180)=3\pi/4$ radians.

## 4. Choose a ratio

Relative to angle $\theta$, the adjacent leg is 7 and hypotenuse is 25. Find $\theta$ in degrees.

**Feedback:** $\cos\theta=7/25$, so $\theta=\arccos(7/25)\approx73.74^\circ$.

## Recovery route

- Wrong side labels: name the reference angle before opposite and adjacent.
- Lengths added instead of squared: state whether they are collinear or perpendicular.
- Area or volume scaled linearly: apply the scale factor once per dimension.
- Inverse function confused with reciprocal: write `arc` notation explicitly.
- Plausible digits but wrong angle: label degrees or radians before interpreting.
